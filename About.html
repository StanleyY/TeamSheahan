About Us

Hunterdon Ski and Snowboard is your one stop Ski and Snowboard shop.

Embrace winter!! Hunterdon Ski and Snowboard is New Jersey's most family friendly ski and snowboard premier sales and rental shop. Discover great savings on everything you will need to make your trip to the mountain a warm, safe and fun one. We offer the finest in skis, snowboards, boots, bindings, poles, goggles and helmets. We sell all major brands including Fischer, Dynastar, Head, Lange, Icelantic, Armada, Ride, GNU, Salomon, Flow, Scott, Bern, Uvex, Spy and others. We also offer a wide selection of the latest fashions from the industry finest manufactures including: Ride, Sessions, Grenade, Marker, Betty Ride, RipZone, Under Armour, Roxy, and Quicksilver. Check out our professional and fully trained and certified ski and snowboard tuning and custom repair shop. Stop by today and experience the difference.
